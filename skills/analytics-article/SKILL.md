# Analytics Article (Output)

## What this skill does

Turns a research bundle into a polished long-form article with a clear narrative arc, evidence grounding, and embedded charts.

## Structure

1) **Headline + dek**
2) **TL;DR** (5 bullets)
3) **The setup** (context + why now)
4) **The core mechanism** (how it works; technical explanation)
5) **What the data says** (charts + interpretation)
6) **Case studies** (2–4 vignettes)
7) **Risks / failure modes**
8) **What to watch next** (measurable indicators)
9) **Methods + provenance appendix**
10) **Citations**

## Embedded charts

Include charts as either:

- Mermaid (flows / diagrams), or
- Inline CSV data blocks + “render hint” (x/y fields, aggregation, window).

## Style rules

- Neutral, precise tone; avoid hype.
- Use the accent color conceptually in branding outputs, not in prose.
- Prefer concrete numbers, timestamps, and entity identifiers.
